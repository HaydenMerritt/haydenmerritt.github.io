"The Forager" project

"The Forager" was one of my first projects, being a college project, but still stands out as one of my best so far, even if it does look like a terraria bootleg. This is because of the procedural world generation, with it I've managed to make a unique world each attempt with working combat, additionally you can break down trees and it drops items. All of this honestly impressed me with the work I did.

"Survival Horror Game"

The Survival Horror game project was another college project, but again stood out as it granted me the opportunity to try my blender assets in a genuine scene, blending it with lighting and make an AI that chases you around the map.
